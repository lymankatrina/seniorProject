Target Audience

This website will serve residents of San Juan County and visitors to the Four Corners area. The target audience is people who enjoy watching movies, and people who are looking for activities to enjoy while visiting Blanding. This includes tourists and local residents. People who live locally will enjoy being able to visit this site to find out what movies are coming to the theater, what movie premiers and special events have been announced, and have the ability to purchase tickets in advance. Surveys allowing for input and feedback will also be appreciated.

Persona 1

This person is a movie buff. He is in high school and follows several well known movie critics. He has strong opinions about what producers and directors are good, and what movies on the horizon look promising. He wants to have access to what movies are coming to San Juan Theater, and would love to provide feedback or recommendations to the owner.

Persona 2

This person is a young parent. She is always looking for activities that will strengthen her family relationships and friendships with others. She enjoys quality entertainment and loves theater popcorn. She will be interested in finding out about matinee events and summer movie specials for her children.

Persona 3

This person is in his sixties. He loves San Juan County and invites his friends and family to visit the area as often as possible. He also loves to take his adult children and grandchildren to the movies. He will be interested in seeing what movies are coming to the theater as he plans how to spend time with his visitors. He will be happy to be able to purchase tickets online and to share the website with those who will be coming to the area.

Scenarios

Visitors can find a list of upcoming premiers, specials, and events.
Visitors can find what movie is now playing with showtimes
Visitors can find what movies will be coming soon.
Visitors can select their seats and purchase tickets online.
Visitors can provide feedback by completing surveys.
Visitors can read about the history of the movie theater.
Visitors can find contact information for the movie theater.
