Color Scheme:

I extracted the colors from the neon sign image on the San Juan Theater facebook page. I used color principles of design to find shades of black, white, gray, and one additional color, blue to complete the color scheme. I used a web accessibility evaluation tool called WAVE to inspect the color scheme for contrast issues to make sure that the color combinations are valid.
