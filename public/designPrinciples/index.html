Site Name: San Juan Movie Theater

Site Purpose:

The purpose of the San Juan Movie Theater website is to provide the residents and visitors of San Juan County with current and upcoming movie showtimes, ticketing information, news updates, theater events, and how to contact the theater. There will also be surveys available for patrons to provide feedback to the theater.

Available Domain Name: "https://sanjuantheater.onrender.com"

Logo:

I created an svg of the neon theater sign. I used the stack of movie reels from the theater's facebook page as inspiration and created an svg of some film reels with a film strip trailing out to the right. I added the tag line: "Follow Me To The Movies" above the film strip.
