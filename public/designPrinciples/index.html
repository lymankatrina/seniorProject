Site Name: San Juan Theater

Site Purpose:

The purpose of the San Juan Movie Theater website is to provide the residents and visitors of San Juan County with current and upcoming movies, showtimes, ticketing information, theater news & events, and how to contact the theater. There will also be surveys available for patrons to provide feedback to the theater.

Available Domain Name: "https://sanjuantheater.onrender.com"

Logos:

I created an svg of the neon theater sign. I used the stack of movie reels from the theater's facebook page as inspiration and created an svg of some film reels with a film strip trailing out to the right. I added the tag line: "Follow Me To The Movies" above the film strip. I created a beam of light following the same angle as the San Juan text to give the impression of a projected image. I added a glow on the black background for style.

Icons:

I designed three icons for the theater, drawing inspiration from photgraphs of actual paper ticket stubs, film projectors, and film reels. I used the text from the theaer logo on two of the icons.
